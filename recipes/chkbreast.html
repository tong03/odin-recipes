Mexican-Style Air-Fry Chicken Breast

High-protein, healthy, and easy dish that nourish your
body and saves time.

Ingredients

  • 4 extra-long toothpicks
  • 4 teaspoons chili powder, divided
  • 4 teaspoons ground cumin, divided
  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 2 teaspoons chipotle flakes

Procedures

  1. Place toothpicks in a small bowl and cover with water; let them soak to keep
    them from burning while cooking.
  2. Mix 2 teaspoons chili powder and 2 teaspoons cumin in a shallow dish.
  3. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  4. Place chicken breast on a flat work surface. Slice horizontally through the middle.
    Pound each half using a kitchen mallet or rolling pin until about 1/4-inch thick.
